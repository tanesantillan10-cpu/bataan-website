Conservation

A sanctuary for the sea's gentle voyagers.

Nagbalayong, Morong

Pawikan Conservation Center

Located in Barangay Nagbalayong, this center protects endangered marine turtles, especially Olive Ridley and Green Sea Turtles. It serves as a vital hub for marine biodiversity and community-led conservation efforts.

Core Activities:

  • Guided tours of the hatchery for incubated eggs.
  • Seasonal release of baby pawikan (Nov–Feb).
  • Educational programs on marine life.
