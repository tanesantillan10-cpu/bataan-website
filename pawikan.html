Pawikan Center

Protecting the gentle voyagers of the sea.

Nagbalayong, Morong

Pawikan Conservation Center

Established to protect and conserve marine turtles like the Olive Ridley and Green Sea Turtles. This community-led initiative transforms former poachers into protectors of the sea.

Core Activities

  • Hatchery Tours: Learn about turtle eggs and the science of conservation.
  • Turtle Release: Witness baby pawikans return to the sea (Nov–Feb).
  • Marine Education: Programs on biodiversity and environmental protection.
