Balanga Wetland

A sanctuary for migratory wings and coastal life.

Brgy. Tortugas, Balanga City

Balanga Wetland & Nature Park

Recognized as an Important Bird Area (IBA), this protected coastal sanctuary hosts thousands of migratory birds every year. It serves as a vital resting point for species traveling across the globe.

🐦 Birdlife

Egrets, Herons, Wild Ducks, and Terns.

🔭 Tourism Value

Birdwatching towers, boardwalks, and educational exhibits.

Annual Event: Migratory Bird Festival — Celebrating ecotourism and conservation.
