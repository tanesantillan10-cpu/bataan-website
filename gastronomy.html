Signature Dishes

Explore the flavors of the Bataan Peninsula.

Tinumis

A local version of dinuguan, made with pork and vinegar, often served with rice.

Adobong Pugita

Octopus cooked adobo-style, reflecting Bataan’s coastal abundance.

Pancit Bataan

Stir-fried noodles with seafood and vegetables.

Fresh Seafood

Crabs, prawns, and fish from Morong and Mariveles, often grilled or cooked in coconut milk.

Suman sa Ibos

Sticky rice delicacy wrapped in palm leaves, a common pasalubong.
