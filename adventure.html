Hiking Adventure

Conquer the rugged peaks and forested trails of Bataan.

Mariveles, Bataan

Pantingan Peak

For those seeking a more challenging and less crowded escape, Pantingan Peak offers an adventurous trek through dense, forested paths. It is part of the Mariveles mountain range, known for its steep ascents and raw natural beauty.

Difficulty: Major Climb
Trail: Forested / Mossy
Trekker's Note: This trail is best suited for experienced hikers. Local guides are highly recommended due to the technical nature of the path.
